    • Summary: The AGI timeline debate pits futurists (e.g., Musk, Altman) predicting AGI within years against pragmatists (e.g., VCs like James Newell) who prioritize vertical AI for immediate value. This reflects speculative vs. practical priorities.

    • Tone and Patterns: Polarized, with utopian AGI rhetoric clashing against grounded, results-driven narratives. X amplifies both sides, creating fragmentation.
